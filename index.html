Rapid Testing Framework

In this project I was tasked to create a research and test framework for large enterprise company to validate assumption and test new features and POCs (Proof of Concept) within 24/48hours turnaround.

While most of large companies still follow waterfall methodology, in this case we have decided to gather ideas from all across business and users. These were put through RTS allowing to quickly triage ideas based on feasibility, real-time user feedback and impact on various KPIs.

In weekly runs decisions are made on which ideas will be eliminated, ”parked” for the moment and which will be pushed to further agile production cycle.
